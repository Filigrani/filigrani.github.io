Belts elements

Elements:

<png> - This image of upper belt on the waist of the protagonist. Images templates you can find on this site in tab "Image templates"
<lowerbelt> - This lower belt image for this belt.
<slot> - This name of slot for equiping, in this case should be "Belt"
<armor> - This how many armor give this belt.
